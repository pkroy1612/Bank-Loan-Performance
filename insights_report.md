## 2. Risk Assessment Findings

### Default Rate Analysis

🚨 **Key Finding:** Overall default rate of **13.8%** is within industry norms but varies significantly by segment.

**Default Rate by Grade:**
- Grade A: **5.2%** (Low Risk)
- Grade B: **8.7%** (Acceptable Risk)
- Grade C: **12.3%** (Moderate Risk)
- Grade D: **17.8%** (High Risk) ⚠️
- Grades E-G: **24.5%** (Very High Risk) 🔴

### Good vs Bad Loans

✅ **Good Loans (Fully Paid + Current):**
- Count: 33,243 loans (**86.2%**)
- Funded Amount: $370.2M
- Received Amount: $414.5M
- Net Profit: $44.3M

❌ **Bad Loans (Charged Off):**
- Count: 5,333 loans (**13.8%**)
- Funded Amount: $65.5M
- Recovered Amount: $58.6M
- Total Loss: $6.9M

### Critical Risk Factors

1. **High DTI Correlation:**
   - DTI > 25%: Default rate of 22.1%
   - DTI < 15%: Default rate of 7.8%
   - **Recommendation:** Implement stricter DTI caps

2. **Verification Impact:**
   - Not Verified: 18.4% default rate
   - Verified: 10.2% default rate
   - **Recommendation:** Mandate verification for grades C and below

3. **Geographic Risk Variation:**
   - Lowest default states: CA (9.2%), NY (10.1%)
   - Highest default states: MS (21.3%), LA (19.7%)
   - **Recommendation:** Adjust pricing by geography
